OUR STORY

Edward, Siyabulela, Rangani, Rooi, and Tshepang make up a close-knit team of young men who first crossed paths while studying IT at university. They share a strong interest in all things tech and decided to come together to tackle a challenging project for their coursework: creating an online platform for ordering food. Each one brings something unique to the table. Edward is the coding guru, turning lines of text into the functional heart of their website. Siyabulela has a keen eye for design, making sure the site looks inviting and user-friendly. Rangani, Rooi, and Tshepang join forces to manage the project, with Rangani handling organization, Rooi bringing marketing flair, and Tshepang keeping a watchful eye on the budget. Together, they make a formidable team, dedicated to their shared goal of making food accessible at the click of a button.



OUR TEAM

Tshepang (Visionary and Project Manager)
Role: Tshepang takes on the role of the visionary and project manager. He's responsible for setting the overall vision and goals for the food e-commerce website project. He also oversees project planning, resource allocation, and ensures that the team stays aligned with the project's objectives.

Edward (Lead Developer)
Role: Edward is the lead developer and head of the technical team. His primary responsibility is to oversee the development of the website. He manages coding, programming, and ensures the technical aspects of the platform are robust and efficient.

Siyabulela (User Experience Designer)
Role: Siyabulela serves as the user experience (UX) designer. His focus is on creating an intuitive and visually appealing interface for the website. Siyabulela ensures that the platform is user-friendly, engaging, and provides an excellent user experience.

Rooi (Marketing and Promotion Specialist)
Role: Rooi takes on the role of marketing and promotion specialist. He's responsible for devising marketing strategies to attract users and partner with local eateries. Rooi also manages promotional campaigns, social media presence, and customer engagement.

Rangani (Cybersecurity and Data Protection Expert)
Role: Rangani is the cybersecurity and data protection expert. His primary duty is to secure the website, user data, and payment information from potential threats. Rangani implements security protocols and ensures the platform complies with data protection regulations.




Vision and Mission

Vision

Our vision is to create a seamless online marketplace where customers can easily access and enjoy a diverse range of high-quality breakfast, lunch, and dinner options from the comfort of their homes. We aim to be the go-to destination for individuals and families seeking delicious and convenient meal solutions. By offering a wide assortment of culinary delights, we aspire to make every mealtime a delightful and stress-free experience for our customers, enhancing their daily lives through the joy of food.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly e-commerce platform that delivers fresh and delectable breakfast, lunch, and dinner options at the tap of a button. We are dedicated to collaborating with local and trusted food providers to ensure the highest quality, variety, and affordability in our offerings. Our commitment to efficient delivery services and a focus on customer satisfaction drives us to simplify meal planning and make wholesome, flavorful dining accessible to all, promoting a healthier and more convenient way of living.
